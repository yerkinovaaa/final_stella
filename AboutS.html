About!

Why Italian Restaurant?

The plan to open an Italian restaurant has been under construction for several years. The initial purpose of the opening was to show the residents of Kazakhstan the masterpieces of Italian cuisine and make them feel like they are in Italy. Especially for this, our chefs have unlearned their skills from famous Italian chefs and are ready to delight you with their masterpieces.

I hope with us you can feel like in Italy. Bon Appetit!

From our chef:

Coming to us for a lunch or a dinner should feel just as comfortable, as having one at home. The simplicity, freshness and reasonable pricing for our dishes just underlines how home-like our Restaurant is.
Going to a restaurant is one of my keenest pleasures. Meeting someplace with old and new friends, ordering wine, eating food, surrounded by strangers, I think is the core of what it means to live a civilised life.
